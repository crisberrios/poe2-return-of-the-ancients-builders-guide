## Most BOOSTED (biggest patch-driven upgrades)

| Rank | Ascendancy | What changed | Why this is up |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Gemling Legionnaire** | Advanced Thaumaturgy reworked — every socketed skill gets a quality-based additional effect. Essence of Virtue new defensive notable. | Effectively a unique power source no other class has. Theoretical S-tier from prior F. |
| 2 | **Spirit Walker** (NEW) | Tame Beast buffed (84% more damage at gem 20); 19 new Companion passives; Trusted Kinship rework. Sub-3:30 campaign clears reported. | Brand-new ascendancy, explicitly protected from nerfs by GGG. |
| 3 | **Martial Artist** (NEW) | Tempest Bell triple-bell rework + Gathering Storm shockwave + Hollow Form clones + Way of the Stone Fist unarmed. | Three viable archetypes day one; explicitly protected from nerfs. |
| 4 | **Deadeye** | Mirage Deadeye can use Channelled Skills (Snipe). Wing Blast +25%. Snipe top-end strong. | Most popular Mirage build now auto-channels Snipe = single-target burst with zero risk. |
| 5 | **Tactician** | Banner radius 4.5→6m and no MS penalty. Mirage Archer + Channelled. Elemental Conflux buffed. | Multiple direct buffs to signature skills. |
| 6 | **Smith of Kitava** | Ancestral Bond keystone rework + Minion Splash support + Chober Chaber rework + Verisium runeforging system. | Manifest Weapon Smith is the patch's sleeper minion pick. |
| 7 | **Warbringer (totem variant)** | Ancestral Bond keystone rework (totem builds escape the "lockout" tax). Bonded gloves warcry CDR. Soul Mantle now +75 Spirit. | Totem-leaning Warbringer is the patch's hidden Warrior winner. |
| 8 | **Chronomancer** | Major ascendancy rework — Phased Form (new defensive notable), Footprints in the Sand cast time 0.5→0.1s, 20% Echo/Repeat from Now and Again, faster Sands of Time. | Biggest tier-up potential from a previously bottom-tier ascendancy. |

## Most NERFED

| Rank | Ascendancy | What changed | Why this is down |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Pathfinder** | Overwhelming Toxicity 35→50% less poison duration. Poisonburst Arrow quality cut. Toxic Growth limit 12→5. Lightning Arrow nerf. Two-Hand projectile +Level nerf. | Stacked nerfs gut poison Pathfinder, the dominant 0.4 endgame archetype. |
| 2 | **Acolyte of Chayula** | Massive ES recharge nerfs across every notable. Small ES passives 15→6%. ES recharge rate small passives removed. | Acolyte's ES identity was the patch's biggest indirect victim. |
| 3 | **Blood Mage (Comet variant)** | Comet damage cut 5%+; Vaal Pact rework removes instant leech; small leech passives 10→8%. | Loses its glass-cannon Comet identity. Spark variants survive better. |
| 4 | **Warbringer (bleed variant)** | Voracious removed instant leech; Goring loses 40% phys; Fortifying Cry shockwave limited. (Rolling Slam now a small QoL buff per community testing — was initially read as a huge nerf.) | Bleed Warbringer's identity is hit through the defensive layer (Voracious) and damage nodes, not the slam itself. |
| 5 | **Disciple of Varashta (oil-ignite variant)** | Ignites from oil ground can't compound — kills oil-grenade-ignite-chain build. | Other variants survive but the strongest 0.4 carry build is gone. |

## League-START tier list (softcore trade — predicted)

### S-Tier — Top picks
- **Spirit Walker** (NEW) — Stag Stampede campaign + Bear endgame. Fresh ascendancy, GGG-protected.
- **Martial Artist** (NEW) — Tempest Bell triple-bell + Hollow Form clones. Three viable archetypes.
- **Deadeye** — Ice Shot → Snipe + Mirage Deadeye. Strongest projectile carry.
- **Titan** — Earthquake Titan or Shield Wall. Reliable, defenses intact, Hulking Form untouched.
- **Tactician** — War Banner + Wolf Companion. Multiple direct buffs.
- **Gemling Legionnaire** — high-variance theorycrafter pick; quality-additional-effects mechanic is wild.

### A-Tier — Strong picks
- **Lich** — Skeletal Army + Archon of Undeath cluster + Chober Chaber rework.
- **Smith of Kitava** — Manifest Weapon. Sleeper minion-warrior. Excellent HC pick.
- **Warbringer** (totem variant) — Ancestral Bond rework + warcry CDR gloves.
- **Witchhunter** — Grenade Mercenary. Sorcery Ward nerf real but Mirage Archer + Snipe synergy compensates.
- **Oracle** — Shapeshift Oracle (Bear/Wolf). Pounce + Comet variants slightly down.
- **Blood Mage** (Spark variant) — Spark + Orb of Storms. Avoids Comet nerf.
- **Invoker** — Tempest Bell Invoker. Strong burst once geared.

### B-Tier — Decent picks
- **Stormweaver** — Spark caster. Stable but unremarkable.
- **Shaman** — Bear-form Shaman. Wyvern's Breath rework helps; Pounce nerfed.
- **Disciple of Varashta** — Djinn invocations. Lost oil-ignite carry.
- **Amazon** — Ice/Tornado Shot. Two-Hand +Level nerf real.
- **Infernalist** — Demon Form leveling, Skeletal Storm Mage endgame.
- **Acolyte of Chayula** — ES nerfs hurt but Into the Breach buffed.

### C-Tier — Avoid for league start
- **Pathfinder** — Poison gutted; only Lightning/Grenade variants viable. Wait for next patch.
- **Chronomancer** — Improved but still niche.
- **Ritualist** — Gear-dependent; great late, poor early.
- **Abyssal Lich** — Use regular Lich instead.

## Top 3 League Starter Recommendations (softcore trade)

### 🥇 Spirit Walker — Stag Stampede / Bear Companion
- **Why:** Brand-new ascendancy, GGG-protected from nerfs, fastest campaign clears reported (sub-3:30). Tame Beast is build-defining and now 84% stronger at gem 20.
- **Risk:** High variance — week-1 meta may stabilize differently. New ascendancies often get tuned post-launch.
- **Path:** [spirit-walker.md](@/spirit-walker.md)

### 🥈 Deadeye — Ice Shot → Snipe + Mirage Deadeye
- **Why:** The biggest single-skill buff of the patch (Mirage + Channelled Skills) targets Deadeye's most-used skill. Strong campaign + S-tier endgame.
- **Risk:** Two-Hand projectile +Level nerf is real. Lightning Arrow variants worse.
- **Path:** [deadeye.md](@/deadeye.md)

### 🥉 Titan — Earthquake Titan or Shield Wall Titan
- **Why:** Most reliable Warrior. Hulking Form + Ancestral Empowerment + Mountain Splitter all untouched. Earthquake aftershock directly buffed. The "boring but works" pick.
- **Risk:** Two-Hand +Level item nerf hurts gear scaling. (Rolling Slam-centric variants are fine — the skill is now a small QoL buff after community testing.)
- **Path:** [titan.md](@/titan.md)

## Honorable mentions

- **Tactician** — for players who like banner-buffs + ranged. War Banner radius buff is a clean upgrade.
- **Smith of Kitava (Manifest Weapon)** — for hardcore players or those who like minion-warrior fantasy. The Verisium runeforging mechanic means unique-weapon-dependent builds can scale early.
- **Lich** — for SSF players. Permanent skeleton army + spectre + new Archon of Undeath cluster.

## How to use this research

1. **Pick a class fantasy.** Bow/spear ranged → Spirit Walker / Deadeye. Caster → Spark Blood Mage / Stormweaver. Melee → Titan / Smith. Minion → Lich / Smith / Spirit Walker. Tank → Smith / Warbringer / Witchhunter.
2. **Read the per-ascendancy file** for that class to see its specific patch impact, top builds, and source guides (EN/CN/KR).
3. **Check the linked Mobalytics / Maxroll guides** for full build details.
4. **Check the [repository](https://github.com/crisberrios/poe2-return-of-the-ancients-builders-guide) for updates.** Once poe.ninja shows Runes of Aldur data, the popularity-% numbers in each file will be updated to reflect actual 0.5 meta rather than the 0.4 baseline. Pull updates from the repo to get the latest revisions.
