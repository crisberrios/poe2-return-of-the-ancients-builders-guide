## Identity

Witch alt-Lich variant introduced post-0.3. Abyss-themed dark magic / poison hybrid. Less focused on minions than original Lich, more on chaos and abyss-jewel-stacking.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Abyssal_Lich)):
- Abyss Jewel synergy notables.
- Chaos damage scaling.
- Bone/death-themed sustain.

## Net verdict — Abyssal Lich

- **0.4 tier:** B− (very niche).
- **0.5 verdict:** B−. Same — niche but not bad.
- **Direction:** Roughly flat.
