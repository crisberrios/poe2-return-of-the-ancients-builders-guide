**Patch:** 0.5.0 "Return of the Ancients" · **Class:** Monk

> 0.4 ninja data; Runes of Aldur not yet indexed.

## Identity

Chaos/darkness Monk ascendancy. Uses "Flames of Chayula" mechanic — pick up red/blue flames for life/mana leech burst.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Acolyte_of_Chayula)):
- **Waking Dream** — Into the Breach skill: pick up flames → leech max life/mana.
- **Hand of Chayula** — chaos damage skill.
- **Mother of the Void** — chaos resistance + damage.
- ES-based damage scaling (the patch's biggest energy-shield-nerf victim).

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A1: Into the Breach — Red Flame leech 15→20% max life; Blue Flame leech 15→20% max mana; wording clarified** | **+ M** | Direct buff to the 59% pick-rate signature skill. |
| **T11/T27/T29: Massive ES recharge nerfs across the board** | **− L** | **Acolyte's ES-based identity takes the worst beating of any ascendancy.** |
| S15: Ghost Dance changes | mixed | Defensive impact. |
| S18: Ice Strike quality | − S | If used. |

### Aggregate: **net negative (−0.6)** — Into the Breach buff is real but doesn't offset the ES recharge mass nerfs that gut Acolyte's defensive engine.

---

## Net verdict — Acolyte of Chayula

- **0.4 tier:** F (3rd-party tier list rated it F-tier for league start).
- **0.5 verdict:** **D**. Slight up from F because of Into the Breach buff, but ES nerfs hit hard.
- **Direction:** Mixed (signature skill buffed, defensive layer gutted).
- **League-start viability:** **Avoid for league start.** The ES nerfs make early gearing painful and the build doesn't scale as well as other monks.

**Recommended starter path:** Not recommended for league start. If committed, consider evasion-based defense over pure ES stacking.
