> 0.4 ninja data; Runes of Aldur not yet indexed.

## Identity

Huntress ascendancy focused on evasion + ranged projectile crit. **#9 popularity SC (4.3%).** Bow/spear hybrid.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Amazon)):
- **Stalking Panther** — doubles evasion from gloves/boots/helms; halves body armour evasion. Massive evasion stack.
- **Mystic Harvest** — life recovery rate per missing life.
- **Trinity** — synergy with multi-element damage.
- Crit-enabling notables.

---

## Net verdict — Amazon

- **0.4 tier:** S (3rd-party tier list predicted Amazon S-tier for league start).
- **0.5 verdict:** B+. The Two-Hand projectile +Level nerf and Lightning Arrow nerf are real hits. Still functional but less dominant.
- **Direction:** Slight down.
- **League-start viability:** **Strong** — Amazon retains its evasion + crit identity and good single-target.

**Recommended starter path:** Ice Shot or Tornado Shot Amazon with Stalking Panther evasion stack.
