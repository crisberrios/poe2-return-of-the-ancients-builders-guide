## Identity

Life-based spellcaster who pays in HP to deal massive damage. Casts spells from life instead of mana, scales spell damage via leech and life pool. **The #1 most popular ascendancy in 0.4 SC** at 17.1%.

**Signature ascendancy notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Blood_Mage)):
- **Vitality Siphon** — leeches a % of Spell Damage as Life (now 20%, up from 10% in 0.5 — a direct buff).
- **Crimson Power** — % of spell damage paid with life instead of mana.
- **Sanguine Conjuration / Iron Reflexes** — life and conversion synergy.
- Various scaling notables for spell damage from life pool.

### Build archetypes

1. **Comet Blood Mage** — burst single-target with massive crit Comets. Hit by Comet damage nerf (#S4 in matrix).
2. **Spark Blood Mage** — wide-screen lightning clear via chain Spark + Orb of Storms triggers.
3. **Lightning Conduit Blood Mage** — Comet → Lightning Conduit detonation combo.
4. **Essence Drain / Contagion Blood Mage** — chaos DoT variant (less common than Lich's).

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change (from matrix) | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A2: Vitality Siphon 10→20% spell leech** | + M | Direct buff to Blood Mage's core leech notable. |
| **S4: Comet damage trimmed at top end** | − M | Comet was 74% of Blood Mages! Top-gem damage cut ~5%. Still very strong but no longer overtuned. |
| **T6: Vaal Pact rework — no more instant leech; 50% more leech amount but 67% less leech speed** | **− L** | Major identity shift. Glass-cannon Blood Mages relied on instant leech to face-tank. Now must rely on life pool + sustained leech. |
| T11/T27/T29: ES recharge nerfs | ~ | Blood Mage doesn't use ES — neutral. |
| T22: Casting Cascade now scales over 8s (was 4s) | + S | Sustained casters benefit. |
| T26: Witch/Sorceress starter spell damage 8→10% | + S | Direct buff. |
| T28: Small "amount of Life Leeched" 8% (was 10%) | − S | Minor leech nerf. |
| S30: Rolling Magma chains 3→5 | + S | If you use Rolling Magma. |

### Aggregate: roughly **flat (−0.6 net)** but with major **playstyle change**. Vitality Siphon buff offsets Comet nerf. Vaal Pact rework forces builds away from "no flask, no need" instant-leech designs.

---

## Net verdict — Blood Mage

- **0.4 tier:** S (#1 popularity).
- **0.5 verdict:** A. Still very strong, but no longer the runaway #1. Spark and Lightning Conduit variants likely overtake Comet variants.
- **Direction:** Down slightly in absolute power; relatively down from #1 because the Comet meta is being tuned and instant-leech glass-cannon archetype is dead.
- **Buffs:** Vitality Siphon (#A2), Witch starter spell damage (#T26).
- **Nerfs:** Comet (#S4), Vaal Pact rework (#T6), Small leech (#T28).
- **League-start viability:** **Strong** if you don't try to copy 0.4's instant-leech Comet glass cannon. Spark + Lightning Conduit + life-pool stacking is the safer 0.5 path.

**Recommended starter path:** Spark or Lightning Conduit Blood Mage; skip Comet-centric variants until the meta settles.
