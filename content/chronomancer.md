## Identity

Time-manipulation Sorceress. Cooldown reduction, echo/repeat mechanics, freeze/time-stop interactions. **Heavily reworked in 0.5 — biggest direct ascendancy rework of the patch.**

**Signature notables in 0.5** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Chronomancer)):
- **Now and Again** — reworked: 20% chance to Echo Cascadable Spells, 20% chance to Repeat Repeatable Spells.
- **Unbound Encore** — promoted to take old Now-and-Again slot.
- **Phased Form** (new) — 30% less damage taken; 4s later take 30% of that hit; removes delayed damage when triggered via Footprints in the Sand.
- **Footprints in the Sand** — Temporal Rift cast time 0.5→0.1s; clears Phased Form delayed damage.
- **Inevitability** — Inevitable Agony reworked: Life Loss debuff, instant cull at threshold, 12s duration, 25% of hit damage (was 50%).
- **Quicksand Hourglass** — Sands of Time now grants 1–60% increased Skill Speed (was Cast Speed).
- **Rapid River** — REMOVED.

## Net verdict — Chronomancer

- **0.4 tier:** F (3rd-party tier list rated it bottom: "Pressing extra buttons to kill bosses when you can just kill them with more damage isn't exactly the best way to play").
- **0.5 verdict:** **C+**. The rework is a meaningful identity update. Phased Form + faster Temporal Rift + 20% Echo chance are real upgrades. Still niche.
- **Direction:** **UP** — biggest tier-up potential of any 0.4-bottom ascendancy.
- **League-start viability:** **Improving** but still not a top recommendation. Best for cold spell casters who want big single-target boss kills.

**Recommended starter path:** Time Freeze + Comet + Echo Now-and-Again. Use Footprints + Phased Form for defensive layer.
