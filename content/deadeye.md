> 0.4 ninja data; Runes of Aldur not yet indexed.

## Identity

Bow specialist. Multi-projectile, far-shot, mirror clones. **The strongest bow ascendancy.**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Deadeye)):
- **+1 to Projectiles** (additional arrow notable).
- **Mirage Deadeye** — clone that uses your projectile skills.
- **Endless Munitions** / various projectile multipliers.
- **Twister** — spawns spinning AoE.

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **S22: Mirage Archer / Mirage Deadeye — can now use Channelled Skills (Snipe, Plasma Blast, Detonating Arrow)** | **+ L** | **The patch's biggest single-skill buff for Deadeye.** 59% of Deadeyes already used Mirage Deadeye — now it can channel Snipe automatically while you freely move. |
| **S35: Snipe — base 121–312% (was 133–343%); Explosion 484–1449% (was 454–1358%); Icy Blast 764–3067% (was 913–3062%); Unique-enemy explosion 33–50% more (was always 50%)** | mixed | Net slightly stronger at endgame, slightly weaker in campaign. |

| S17: Ice Shot Ice Shards no longer 25% more Freeze Buildup | − S | Ice Shot variant. |
| S19: Lightning Arrow multiple Beams can't chain to same target | − M | Lightning Arrow Deadeye. |
| I2: Two-hand projectile +Level T1 = +5 (was +7) | − L | All bow Deadeyes. |
| Wind Ward removal from 0.4 (not in 0.5 notes — was 0.4) | ~ | Already baked in. |

### Aggregate: **net positive (+1.5)** — Mirage + Snipe synergy is a major archetype unlock. Two-Hand +Level nerf hurts but doesn't break it.

---

## Net verdict — Deadeye

- **0.4 tier:** A (3rd-party tier list saw it as a recovery from late-0.3).
- **0.5 verdict:** **S (Promoted from A)**. The Mirage + Snipe combination is the biggest new power unlock for any ranged class.
- **Direction:** **UP.** Best-positioned bow ascendancy in 0.5.
- **League-start viability:** **Strong** — Ice Shot leveling → swap to Snipe + Mirage at endgame.

**Recommended starter path:** Ice Shot/Snipe Deadeye with Mirage Deadeye handling Snipe channeling at endgame. The strongest projectile build going into 0.5.
