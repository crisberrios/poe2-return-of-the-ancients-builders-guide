## Identity

Sorceress ascendancy invoking djinn-like spirits. Cold spell caster with summoned invocations and ice-wall mechanics. **The dominant HC and HC SSF ascendancy in 0.4 (14.8% HC, 16.2% HC SSF).**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Disciple_of_Varashta)):
- **Kelari, the Tainted Sand** — summons a sand spirit.
- **Navira, the Last Mirage** — summons a mirage spirit.
- **Varashta's Promise** — sustains spirit invocations.
- Cold-conversion notables.

---

## Net verdict — Disciple of Varashta

- **0.4 tier:** S (hardcore especially). B+ SC.
- **0.5 verdict:** **B**. Drops because the oil-ignite carry build is dead, but invocation/djinn playstyle is intact.
- **Direction:** Down for oil-ignite builds, stable for djinn-invocation builds.
- **League-start viability:** **Still strong, especially HC.** Djinn invocations weren't directly touched.

**Recommended starter path:** Lily's Djinn Sorceress (Kelari + Navira) is the safest carry build. Avoid oil-grenade-ignite.
