## Identity

Attribute-stacking Mercenary. Trades flexibility for raw stat scaling and gem-quality manipulation. **Massively reworked in 0.5 — one of the biggest direct ascendancy buffs of the patch.**

**Signature notables in 0.5** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Gemling_Legionnaire)):
- **Essence of Virtue** (NEW notable) — grants Virtuous Barrier skill: accumulates protective gemstone Motes for each Attribute over time; loses a random Mote when hit.
- **Advanced Thaumaturgy** (reworked) — **Gem Quality grants Socketed Skills an additional effect**. All gems now have an additional quality stat visible holding Alt.
- **Crystalline Potential** — REMOVED.

This rework effectively gives Gemling a **unique power source no other class has** — every socketed skill gets an additional bonus from gem quality.

## Net verdict — Gemling Legionnaire

- **0.4 tier:** F (3rd-party tier list: "There is almost nothing good about this thing at league start. Every build you can play on this, you can play on something else and it will be better.").
- **0.5 verdict:** **A− / S (theoretical)**. The new Advanced Thaumaturgy rework is functionally a unique power source. 3rd-party tier list rates it **S-tier promoted from F**.
- **Direction:** **MASSIVE up.** Biggest tier-up of any ascendancy in 0.5.
- **League-start viability:** **High variance.** If you enjoy theorycrafting and seeing which skills benefit most from the new gem-quality bonus, this is the most rewarding ascendancy to learn in 0.5. If you want a known-good starter, wait a week to see what builds emerge.

**Recommended starter path:** Crossbow/grenade Gemling for safe leveling, then theorycraft high-quality gem stacking once at endgame.
