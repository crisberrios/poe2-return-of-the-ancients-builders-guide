> 0.4 ninja data; Runes of Aldur not yet indexed.

## Identity

Witch's fire-themed ascendancy. Demon Form for caster builds OR Skeletal minion army.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Infernalist)):
- **Demon Form** — transform skill with damage scaling + an excellent dash.
- **Infernal Hound** — companion summon.
- **Beidat's Hand** / fire conversion.
- Hellhound / fire / minion scaling.

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **N1: Minion Splash Strength Support Gems** | + M | Melee minions (Skeletal Warriors/Reavers) can multi-hit. |
| Infernal Legion nerf (20→10% of max life, IL3 removed) | − M | Specifically hurts the Infernal-Legion-Hound playstyle. |
| **T3: Archon of Undeath cluster** | + M | New scaling for Witch minions. |
| SS2: All Command Skills 50% MS penalty (was 70%) | + M | Smoother active minion commanding. |
| S10: Flame Breath energy gain penalty | − M | Affects fire-Infernalist variants. |
| Demon Form is a Shapeshift skill — may get huge benefits from new Druid nodes (mentioned in tier list) | + M | Druid passive cluster crossover. |
| T11: ES recharge nerfs | − M | If you use ES sustain. |

### Aggregate: **mildly positive (+0.3)** — Skeletal builds gain new tools, Demon Form gets shapeshift crossover, Infernal Legion variants nerfed.

---

## Net verdict — Infernalist

- **0.4 tier:** B (consistent mid-popularity, "fastest leveling build" reputation).
- **0.5 verdict:** B+. Demon Form gets new shapeshift synergies; Skeletal Storm Mage remains strong.
- **Direction:** Mild up for Demon Form, mild down for high-HP-minion variants.
- **League-start viability:** **Excellent for leveling** — Infernalist Demon Form is regarded as one of the fastest acts 1–3.

**Recommended starter path:** Demon Form leveling → Skeletal Storm Mage at endgame.
