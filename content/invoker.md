## Identity

Elemental Monk. Converts physical damage to elemental, leverages charges and elemental skills. **#6 most popular in 0.4 SC (5.3%).**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Invoker)):
- **Elemental Expression** — converts physical to elemental.
- **Wei Tide** — block + elemental ailment scaling.
- **Conduction** — shock-related notable.
- Charge-generation and elemental scaling.

## Net verdict — Invoker

- **0.4 tier:** B (mid endgame popularity; gear-dependent).
- **0.5 verdict:** **B+ / A−**. Three-bell Tempest Bell is the most exciting new combo for any quarterstaff user, and Invoker capitalizes on it best with elemental scaling.
- **Direction:** **Up.** Tempest Bell rework is a meaningful buff to Invoker's signature combo.
- **League-start viability:** **Good but gear-dependent.** Toxic Growth + Tempest Bell is a serviceable starter; the bell+combo really shines at endgame with bell-stacking.

**Recommended starter path:** Tempest Bell Invoker with Gathering Storm dash; Lightning Charged Staff backup. The new Martial Artist ascendancy may overshadow Invoker for the pure bell archetype — see [martial-artist.md].
