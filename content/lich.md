## Identity

Premiere dedicated minion ascendancy + chaos DoT specialist. Bypass-life sustain via ES.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Lich)):
- **Unholy Might** — minions deal extra chaos damage.
- **Jewel Socket** — extra jewel slot (rare ability among ascendancies).
- **Bone Cage / Death Ward** — defensive ES-based mechanics.
- Minion permanence and scaling notables.

## Net verdict — Lich

- **0.4 tier:** A (HC especially S; SSF top-3 due to spectre permanence).
- **0.5 verdict:** **A**. Strongly positioned for minion + chaos DoT. The new Archon cluster + Minion Splash + Chober Chaber rework are all in Lich's wheelhouse.
- **Direction:** Stable to mildly up.
- **League-start viability:** **Excellent**, particularly for HC and SSF where minion permanence shines.

**Recommended starter path:** ED/Contagion Lich leveling, transition to Skeletal Army + Spectre Lich at endgame.
