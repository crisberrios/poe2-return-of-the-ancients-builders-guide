## Identity

Brand-new Monk ascendancy. Master of illusions — creates illusory clones, summons spiritual bells, excels at hand-as-weapon combat.

Per the GGG patch notes: *"After decades of training, the Martial Artist has become a master of illusions. Empowered by the runes socketed into his body, this class creates powerful illusions, summons illusory bells to aid him, and excels at using his hands as weapons."*

## Signature ascendancy nodes (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Martial_Artist) + 0.5 previews)

### Hollow Techniques (three signature skills)

- **Hollow Form** — channeled skill creating multiple illusory clones at the target location. Clones automatically attack the nearest enemy using your socketed attack skill and vanish after a single attack. **Core damage multiplier of the build.**
- **Hollow Focus** — manifests floating spiritual bells around your character. Bells remain passive until you attack them, triggering immediate AoE damage and crowd-control effects.
- **Hollow Resonance** — spawns a permanent bell attached to your character's back. Every time you land a critical hit with any skill, the bell rings automatically and deals AoE damage.

### Hand-to-Hand combat

- **Way of the Stone Fist** — elevates unarmed combat (gloves-based attack). Per IGGM commentary: *"the most outrageous glove mechanic ever"* — turns bare fists into solid stone for unarmed-only scaling.
- Combat loop is closer to **attack-building → charging → detonation** (Diablo-2-style) than pure martial-arts spam.

## Expected build archetypes (early 0.5)

1. **Tempest Bell + Triple Bell Martial Artist** — leverages the new 3-bell cap on Tempest Bell (was 1). The signature 0.5 combo. Best paired with quarterstaff + Gathering Storm dash.
2. **Hollow Form Clone Burst Martial Artist** — illusion-clone burst with a high-damage attack skill socketed. Each clone is one extra strike per cast.
3. **Way of the Stone Fist (Hollow Palm) Martial Artist** — unarmed monk. Carries the Hollow Palm fantasy from PoE1 with a fresh design.
4. **Critical-trigger Hollow Resonance Martial Artist** — crit-stacking build using Hollow Resonance bells as ambient AoE.

## Net verdict — Martial Artist

- **0.4 tier:** N/A (didn't exist).
- **0.5 verdict (preliminary):** **S**. 3rd-party tier list articles rank it S-tier alongside Spirit Walker. The signature Tempest Bell triple-bell combo + Hollow Form clone amplification + Hollow Palm unarmed are three distinct strong archetypes.
- **Direction:** Brand-new with very strong early indications. GGG explicitly stated they protected the new ascendancies from nerfs.
- **League-start viability:** **Highly recommended.** Tempest Bell route is the safest carry; Hollow Palm is for theorycrafters.

**Recommended starter path:** Tempest Bell + Gathering Storm Martial Artist using a quarterstaff. Toxic Growth + Tempest Bell makes a strong leveling combo, transitioning to triple-bell endgame.
