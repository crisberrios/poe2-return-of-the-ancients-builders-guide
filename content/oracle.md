## Identity

Druid ascendancy. Hybrid caster-shapeshifter using prophetic visions. **#2 most popular in 0.4 SC (15.8%).**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Oracle)):
- **Oracle's Fateful Vision** — grants Align Fate (visage mechanic).
- **Visions of Fate** — boss-killing burst from accumulated visions.
- Companion / pet scaling nodes.

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Align Fate (Oracle's Fateful Vision skill) — now scales visage frequency with CDR | + S | QoL clarity on existing mechanic. |
| S4: Comet damage trimmed | − M | 34% of Oracles used Comet. |
| S26: Pounce cooldown 4.9–4s → 6–5.1s | − M | 33% of Oracles used Pounce. |
| S38: Tame Beast buff (Spirit-Walker-flavoured but available to Oracles via passives) | + S | Hybrid pet builds. |
| S41: Volcano crit base 8% (was 5%) | + S | Fire spell Oracles. |
| T6: Vaal Pact rework | mixed | If you stacked instant leech, down; otherwise neutral. |
| T22: Casting Cascade 8s window | + S | Sustained casters. |
| T26: Witch/Sorceress small spell damage 10% (was 8%) | + S | Druid Oracles routing through Witch start. |

### Aggregate: roughly **flat to mild down (−0.4)**. Pounce nerf + Comet nerf are the visible hits.

---

## Net verdict — Oracle

- **0.4 tier:** S. Was the dominant 0.4 endgame ascendancy alongside Blood Mage.
- **0.5 verdict:** A−. Loses some of its top-tier dominance from Pounce CD + Comet nerf, but core mechanics intact.
- **Direction:** Mild down.
- **League-start viability:** **Strong**. Oracle shapeshift mechanics weren't directly nerfed; only the imported caster skills got tuned.

**Recommended starter path:** Shapeshift Oracle (Bear/Wolf) leaning on melee + Lunar Assault rather than caster Oracle leaning on Comet.
