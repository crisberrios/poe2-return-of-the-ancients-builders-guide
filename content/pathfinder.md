> 0.4 ninja data; Runes of Aldur not yet indexed.

## Identity

Bow/poison specialist. Maps flasks and chaos damage. **#3 most popular in 0.4 SC (12.1%)** but **received the biggest direct nerf in 0.5**.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Pathfinder)):
- **Overwhelming Toxicity** — less poison duration, MORE magnitude (got hit hard in 0.5).
- **Running Assault** — reduce MS penalty while using skills (also nerfed).
- **Master Toxicist** — poison scaling.
- **Lightning Mistress** — lightning-shock conversion.

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A5: Overwhelming Toxicity 35→50% less poison duration** | **− L** | Direct nerf to core poison notable. Stacks with other nerfs. |
| **A5: Running Assault 50→30% less MS penalty** | **− S** | Mild MS QoL nerf. |
| **S25: Poisonburst Arrow — poison duration flat 3s (was 3–4.9s); quality 0–10% more (was 0–20%)** | **− M** | 69% of Pathfinders used PBA. Direct hit. |
| **S40: Toxic Growth — fires 5 Pustules (was 4); Limit 5 (was 12); quality +0–1 (was +0–2)** | **mixed** | More base fires but lower cap. Net negative for high-investment builds. |
| **S19: Lightning Arrow — multiple Lightning Beams can't chain to same target** | − M | Lightning Arrow Pathfinder variants hit. |
| S17: Ice Shot Ice Shards no longer 25% more Freeze Buildup | − S | Ice Shot variant. |
| S43: Wing Blast Shockwave +25% | + M | Niche but real buff. |
| I2: Two-hand projectile +Level weapon T1 = +5 (was +7) | − L | All bow/crossbow Pathfinders. |
| SS1: Fixed-projectile skills now benefit from Ricochet III etc. | + M | Glacial Lance Pathfinder variants. |

### Aggregate: **massive net nerf (~−2.8)**. Stacked nerfs: Overwhelming Toxicity + Poisonburst Arrow + Toxic Growth + Lightning Arrow + Two-Hand +Level all hit the typical Pathfinder.

---

## Net verdict — Pathfinder

- **0.4 tier:** S (consistently top-3 popularity).
- **0.5 verdict:** **C**. Biggest tier drop of any ascendancy. 3rd-party articles (aoeah) rate it C-tier with the comment "lost more than 50% of damage."
- **Direction:** **DOWN significantly.** The poison build's core mechanics took ~50% duration cut compounded with quality nerfs.
- **League-start viability:** **Poison Pathfinder is dead for the foreseeable future.** Lightning/Galvanic Shards and Grenade variants remain functional.

**Recommended starter path:** Switch ascendancy entirely, OR play Lightning/Crossbow Pathfinder. Skip poison until a future patch.
