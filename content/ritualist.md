## Identity

Huntress curse/ritual specialist. Scales rings, curses, and ritual mechanics. **#10 popularity SC (4.0%).**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Ritualist)):
- **Master of Rings** — extra ring slots / ring effect.
- **Ritual scaling** notables.
- **Frenzy Charge** generation.
- Curse / blasphemy support.

## Net verdict — Ritualist

- **0.4 tier:** D (3rd-party tier list rated lower for SC league start due to gear-dependent scaling). C in this analysis.
- **0.5 verdict:** C+. Tiers up slightly because Ancestral Bond rework helps the totem variant and Glacial Lance with Ricochet is a fun build.
- **Direction:** Roughly flat with modest opportunities.
- **League-start viability:** **Mediocre as a fresh starter.** Ritualist becomes great once you have Kalandra's Touch and Ingenuity, but those are endgame items.

**Recommended starter path:** **NOT a recommended league starter.** If you want Ritualist, level as a Huntress or different ascendancy first, then respec.
