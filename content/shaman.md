## Identity

Druid bear/shapeshift specialist. Rage generation, primal forms, melee tank. **#4 most popular in 0.4 SC (7.7%).**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Shaman)):
- **Adaptation** — self-hit + rage generation synergies.
- **Wild Bond** / **Trusted Kinship** access — companion/pet scaling.
- **Wyvern's Breath** — elemental ailment application + freeze buildup for shapeshifters.
- Rage-conversion / cost-reduction notables.

## Net verdict — Shaman

- **0.4 tier:** B / B+ (4th most popular, but lower than caster classes due to slower clear).
- **0.5 verdict:** B. Roughly stable. Bear-form gets new toys (Wyvern's Breath rework, Companion passives, Volcano crit) while Wolf-form Pounce gets nicked.
- **Direction:** Roughly flat, slight reshuffling within Shaman builds.
- **League-start viability:** **Strong** — Shaman remains one of the most beginner-friendly shapeshift options.

**Recommended starter path:** Bear-form Shaman (Walking Calamity + Maul + Lunar Assault) using Wyvern's Breath for freeze layer. The Companion passive additions are a sleeper buff.
