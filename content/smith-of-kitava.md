## Identity

The crafting-oriented Warrior. Bends weapons to its will (elemental conversion), summons Manifest Weapon minions, and "tanks first, deals damage later."

**Signature ascendancy notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Smith_of_Kitava)):
- **Manifest Weapon** — your damage spawns weapon minions that fight for you. Defines an entire minion-warrior playstyle.
- **Twisted Imperium** — converts Mace fire damage to cold (the Mesa build's foundation).
- **Coal Stoker** / **Forged in Flame** — fire-resistance + conversion supports; lets you cap all elemental resistances around 90% by stacking fire res only.
- **Anvil's Weight**-style armour-break support.
- Various Glory generation and weapon-base scaling nodes.

### Build archetypes

1. **Fire-Bear Smith of Kitava (Balrog)** — Druid bear shapeshift + Fire Spell on Hit (Living Bomb) + Cast on Elemental Ailment (fire-infused Comet). [Mobalytics](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/builds/balrog-fire-bear-smith).
2. **Mesa / Twisted Imperium Cold Conversion** — uses Twisted Imperium to convert Mace fire → cold, scaling shatter clear. Discussed across multiple 0.5 tier-list articles as Smith's "signature" endgame build.
3. **Shield Wall + Resonating Shield Tank** — tank-focused league-starter using stacked armour. [Mobalytics](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/builds/shield-wall-league-starter).
4. **Flame Breathing Smith** — fire DoT focus with Flame Breath. [Mobalytics](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/builds/fire-dragon-smith-of-kitava).
5. **Sunder Smith of Kitava (0.3-era)** — classic ground-slam. [RPGStash 0.3](https://www.rpgstash.com/blog/path-of-exile-2-dawn-of-the-hunt-sunder-smith-of-kitava-build-guide).
6. **Lightning Spear Smith of Kitava** — niche elemental conversion via spear. [poe-vault](https://www.poe-vault.com/poe2/warrior/smith-of-kitava/smith-of-kitava-lightning-spear-build).
7. **Manifest Weapon minion Smith** — the dedicated minion-warrior path. Less popular on ninja but distinctive — and patch 0.5 directly buffs it via the new **Minion Splash Strength support gem** + **Chober Chaber +2-3 to all minion skills** rework.
8. **Zero to Hero (Fire on Hit)** — P4wnyhof guide. [Mobalytics](https://mobalytics.gg/poe-2/profile/p4wnyhof/builds/zero-to-hero-smith-of-kitava).

### Aggregate (rubric: ±L = ±1.0, ±M = ±0.3, ±S = ±0.1)

**Buffs:** +1.0L · +1.2M · +0.6S = **+1.75**
**Nerfs:** +0.0L · −0.3M · −0.4S = **−0.34**
**Net:** **+1.41** (clear net buff — strengthened after Rolling Slam reclassified to a small buff; see `community-notes.md`)

> The 3rd-party tier-list articles (aoeah) calling Smith "A-Tier, promoted from C" lean on these buffs plus the new Verisium runeforging system (which lets you upgrade specific unique weapons — a Smith-friendly mechanic). Verisium is in the "new league mechanics" bucket the user asked to ignore, but it materially affects Smith builds because they rely on specific weapon uniques.

## Net verdict — Smith of Kitava

- **0.4 tier (Mobalytics):** C (SC) / S (HC) — bimodal.
- **0.5 verdict (this analysis):** **B+ (SC) / S (HC).**
- **Direction:** **Mild net buff in SC** through the minion/totem changes; HC viability **stays excellent** because Smith's tankiness wasn't touched.
- **What got buffed:** Manifest Weapon (Chober Chaber #8, Minion Splash #9, new lineage supports #18), Ancestral Bond keystone (#6), AWT delay (#7), Soul Mantle (#10).
- **What got nerfed:** Two-Hand +Level (#5), Voracious instant leech (#11). (Rolling Slam — #1 — reclassified to a small buff; see `community-notes.md`.)
- **League-start viability:** **Strong if you commit to a minion-Smith or tanky league-starter.** Less competitive if you try to play it like a damage-Titan.

**Recommended starter path:** **Manifest Weapon Smith** is the patch's sleeper pick. The combination of Chober Chaber rework + Minion Splash support + new minion lineage supports + the Verisium runeforging system (lets you upgrade weak unique weapons) means a minion Smith can scale into endgame from very budget gear. Alternative: tanky **Shield Wall + Resonating Shield Smith** for hardcore.
