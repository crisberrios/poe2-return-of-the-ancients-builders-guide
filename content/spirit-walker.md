## Identity

Brand-new Huntress ascendancy. Bound to the wilds, summons Azmeri Spirits — Stag, Owl, Bear — each with distinct mechanics. Can tame beast bosses as permanent companions via Tame Beast.

Per the GGG patch notes: *"Bound to the wilds, this class calls upon spirits to answer to her will granting different abilities aligning with the Stag, Owl or Bear. Through their guidance, the Spirit Walker may subdue even the most formidable beasts, binding them in spirit, or call forth a spectral companion to fight beside her."*

## Signature ascendancy nodes (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Spirit_Walker) + 0.5 previews)

Three spirit paths:

### Stag (Vivid Path)
- **Vivid Stampede** — gain a Vivid Wisp every 20m moved, max 3. Expend all Wisps to trigger Vivid Stampede when attacking. Sub-3:30 campaign clear records are reported for Stag.
- **The Morrigan's Guidance** — Stags deal 20% more damage per leap (4 leaps = up to 80% more on the final leap). Grants a Wisp when Stampede ends.

### Owl (Primal Path)
- **The Mhacha's Gift** — dodge consumes all 3 Owl Feathers at once, empowering attacks by 100% per feather. Cuts feather cooldown by 50%. **Widely considered one of the strongest projectile nodes in the ascendancy.**

### Bear (Wild Path)
- **Wild Protector** — permanent spectral bear companion.
- **Catha's Balance** — bear deals additional damage equal to 60% of your main-hand weapon damage.

### Other signature nodes

- **Natural Order** (signature node) — unlocks ability to use **Tame Beast on beast bosses** (Chimeral Beast, Frozen Talon, etc.), turning them into permanent allied combatants. **This is the build-defining mechanic.**
- **Idolatry** — stacking bonuses per equipped Idol: each Idol +10% companion damage, +2% spirit reservation efficiency. Every non-Idol augment applies a 4% elemental resistance penalty.
- **Sacred Unity** — enhances all three spirit paths.

## Expected build archetypes (early 0.5)

1. **Stag Stampede Spirit Walker (campaign speedrun)** — leap-attack physical with stacked Vivid Wisps. Reportedly fastest campaign clear in 0.5.
2. **Owl Projectile Spirit Walker** — dodge-burst empowered projectiles via Mhacha's Gift.
3. **Bear Companion Spirit Walker** — minion-flavored with permanent bear + bear weapon-damage scaling.
4. **Beast-Tamer Spirit Walker** — Natural Order to permanently tame Chimeral Beast or Frozen Talon as your endgame companion.

## Net verdict — Spirit Walker

- **0.4 tier:** N/A (didn't exist).
- **0.5 verdict (preliminary):** **S/A**. 3rd-party tier list articles rank it S-tier with extensive commentary about its sub-3:30 campaign clear records. The patch notes confirm GGG specifically protected Tame Beast from nerfs and buffed it directly.
- **Direction:** Brand-new with strong early indications.
- **League-start viability:** **Highly recommended** as the "most anticipated" 0.5 ascendancy. Caveat — power level may equalize after the first patch tuning pass once GGG sees ladder data.

**Recommended starter path:** Stag (Vivid Stampede) for campaign, transition to Bear (Wild Protector + Catha's Balance) at endgame. Use Natural Order to tame a beast boss as a permanent companion once you can.
