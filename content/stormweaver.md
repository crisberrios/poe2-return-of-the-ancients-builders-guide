## Identity

Elemental Sorceress focused on cold/lightning spells with shock layering. Burst caster archetype. **#7 most popular in 0.4 SC (5.0%).**

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Stormweaver)):
- **Storm Weaver** — bonuses to shocked enemies.
- **Elemental Augmentation** — exposure mechanics.
- **Twofold Shock** — double-shock for party play.
- Cold-conversion and shatter notables.

## Net verdict — Stormweaver

- **0.4 tier:** B (5% popularity but considered solid).
- **0.5 verdict:** B. Roughly stable. Spark variants safer than Comet variants.
- **Direction:** Slightly down.
- **League-start viability:** **Good** — Spark Stormweaver remains one of the smoothest leveling experiences in the game.

**Recommended starter path:** Spark + Orb of Storms Stormweaver. Skip Comet-heavy variants.
