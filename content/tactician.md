## Identity

Mercenary banner/aura specialist. Flexible defensive options across armour, evasion, ES. Crossbow + banner buffs.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Tactician)):
- **A Solid Plan** — less spirit cost reservation (one of the strongest reservation notables).
- **Elemental Conflux** — multi-element scaling.
- **Trinity** — three-element conversion synergy.
- Banner AoE/effect notables.

## Net verdict — Tactician

- **0.4 tier:** A (3rd-party tier list rates it consistent S-tier performer).
- **0.5 verdict:** **S**. Strong winner due to banner buff + Mirage Archer + Conflux + new minion lineage support compatibility (Wolf Companion + War Banner).
- **Direction:** **UP.** Multiple direct buffs to Tactician's signature skills.
- **League-start viability:** **Excellent** — flexible defensive options + immediate damage via Banner+Wolves.

**Recommended starter path:** War Banner + Wolf Companion Tactician, OR Crossbow + Mirage Archer Tactician.
