## Identity

Mercenary crossbow/grenade specialist with anti-magic defensive layer (Sorcery Ward). Tanky ranged.

**Signature notables** (from [poe2db.tw](https://poe2db.tw/us/Witchhunter)):
- **Sorcery Ward** — % less armour/evasion in exchange for spell damage immunity layer.
- **Obsessive Rituals** (Sorcery Ward source) — got nerfed in 0.5.
- **Decisive Strike** / culling crit notables.
- Grenade scaling notables.

## Patch 0.5 impact

| Change | Direction | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A6: Obsessive Rituals (Sorcery Ward) — 50% less armour/evasion (was 35%)** | **− M** | Direct defensive nerf. Endgame builds with high armour totals offset partly. |
| S22: Mirage Archer can now use Channelled Skills | + L | Massive buff for any Witchhunter using Mirage Archer (36% pick!). |
| **S23: Oil Barrage — mana cost -18%; Empowered consumes power charge every 1.5s (was 2s); damage +10/-10%** | mixed | Niche but real change. |
| S12: Fragmentation Rounds quality reworked (+0–2 Fragments instead of more phys) | mixed | If you use Frag. |
| SS4: Crossbow Ammunition skills now have base cost 0 (was disabled) | mixed | Means +cost modifiers now apply. Slight nerf if you used +cost gear by accident. |
| T15: Tempered Mind: 15% inc effect of Fully Broken Armour | + M | Armour-break combo. |

### Aggregate: **mildly positive (+0.2)** — Mirage Archer buff offsets Sorcery Ward nerf for Mirage-leaning builds.

---

## Net verdict — Witchhunter

- **0.4 tier:** A (consistent top-10 popularity, recognized as one of the best campaign classes).
- **0.5 verdict:** **A**. Sorcery Ward nerf is real but Mirage Archer + Snipe synergy (via S22) opens a new powerful endgame archetype.
- **Direction:** Mixed; slight up for Mirage variants, slight down for tank variants.
- **League-start viability:** **Strong** — grenades remain S-tier damage, campaign clear is smooth.

**Recommended starter path:** Grenade Witchhunter or Galvanic Shards Witchhunter; Mirage Archer pickup post-act 6.
